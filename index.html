Background Properties

  • background-color: Solid color backgrounds.
  • background-image: Adding images as backgrounds.
  • background-repeat, background-position: Controlling image repetition and positioning.
  • background-size: Adjusting image size (cover, contain, custom dimensions).
  • background-attachment: Fixed, scroll backgrounds.

Text Effects

  • text-overflow: Handling Text Overflow
  • word-wrap (now overflow-wrap): Breaking Long Words
  • word-break: Controlling Line Breaking for Words
  • writing-mode: Controlling Text Flow Direction
  • text-shadow: Adding shadows to text for depth effects.
  • text-transform: Changing text case
